## Contact Entry Fields

When adding a contact, capture:
- **name** — Full name
- **role** — Job title at TUC RAIL / Infrabel
- **email** — Work email
- **phone** — Work phone (optional)
- **team** — Department/group
- **reports_to** — Direct manager
- **direct_reports** — Who reports to them
- **projects** — Active projects (link to project files)
- **tags** — Searchable keywords (role, specialty, tools)
