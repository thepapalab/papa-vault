# EC7 — Index

Domain index for Eurocode 7 (geotechnical). The most developed methodology domain so far. Sits under [[_Knowledge — Index]].

## Methodology notes

**Settlement — load-induced**
- [[Settlement under a loaded area — calculation methodology]] — *validated to <0.1 mm*. Newmark four-corner stress distribution (Boussinesq n=3 / Buisman n=4) + Terzaghi log compression + Terzaghi consolidation with De Beer stress-dependent cv. The note to use.
- [[Settlement under a loaded area — methodology]] — *tutorial / draft*. Reverse-engineering walkthrough; superseded by the validated note but kept for the derivation.

**Settlement — drawdown-induced**
- [[Dewatering-induced settlement — methodology]] — *draft*. De Beer/Sanglerat compressibility + Terzaghi compression; Dupuit + Sichardt drawdown cone. Third-party impact of dewatering (buoyancy loss), not load. One open validation item (Δσ′ propagation below the drawdown band).

## Salvage maps (build backlogs)

- [[_Pile foundations toolbox — salvage map]] — exhaustive pile & foundation design-check inventory (axial bearing, micropiles, lateral/moment, groups, downdrag, uplift, load tests, driveability, shallow foundations). Includes the dedicated **Bovenleidingspaal** catenary-mast tool.
- [[_Settlement & ground-improvement toolbox — salvage map]] — settlement (elastic + logarithmic families), consolidation acceleration (drains, preload), ground improvement (rigid inclusions, stone columns), monitoring.

## Open threads / gaps noted

- **Catenary foundation checks** (overturning, lateral displacement, bearing under eccentric load) are covered by the Bovenleidingspaal tool, not by the area-settlement tools — see the pile salvage map, batch 4.
- **Characteristics table** NBN EN 1997-1 ANB-2014 Tabel 2.1 carries both the C (Cp′/Cs′) and elastic (Eoed/E50/Eur) parameter sets — feeds either settlement family. Worth a Formulas/ or Norms-Regulations/ entry when that layer is populated.
