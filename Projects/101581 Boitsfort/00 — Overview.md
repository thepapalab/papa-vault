# P101581 — Boitsfort Bardage · Overview

> [!warning] Situation as of 04/06/2026
> **Works stopped since April 2026** — workmanship non-conformities (NC03). Structural technical review in progress by Nicolas: six blocking findings (A–F) in TF-2100 v4.0 (un-recomputed calculation after section change 35×46 → 70×45 mm; incomplete wind methodology; non-representative pull-out test; missing SLS verification; missing ETA 19/0245; unknown substrate grade). Formal response to Equans in preparation via Raphaëlle.

## Project Scope & Design

**P101581 / Veiligheidsuitrusting overdekte sleuf Bosvoorde**

Ventilated timber cladding (Marcovis Eisys A4 screw system + lattage 70×45 C18 + MORA thermally modified cladding 21 mm) on **11 emergency-exit buildings** above the covered trench (NU2–NU8, LS-lokaal A + battery, MS-lokaal A, LS-lokaal B + battery, MS-lokaal B). Pilot: NU5 (Groenendaal direction).

**Fixing system:** Marcovis Eisys A4 façade screws in FM-X3 10/10×80 plugs anchored in AAC (*cellenbetonblokken*) at 80 mm embedment.

**Defining structural parameters:** Section 70×45 mm C18 lattice; exposure class TBD; pull-out resistance at design conditions not yet verified; wind methodology incomplete.
