#### 09/06/2026 — Equans requests meeting; Nicolas holds technical position

Equans (Tim Witvoet) replied 08/06/2026 to Remco (cc De Raedt/Sven Broes, Charles Kint, Hermans, Bataille, Mauroy, Raphaëlle, Raphaël) on the feedback to TF-2101 and TF-2100. Equans considers certain remarks "buitensporig" (excessive) and, to avoid an administrative tangle, proposes a (preferably live) meeting with experts from both sides to align on what administration still has to follow to green-light the two fiches. Proposed dates: Mon 15/06 (≥10h), Thu 18/06, Fri 19/06.

Equans also lists three further items they see as needed to complete the gevelbekleding dossier:
- Adapted or new plans for all buildings (overlaps existing gevelplannen / IP3371 request)
- Technical solution to re-seal the perforations in the gevelfolie at NU5 (waterproofing) — new point I
- Druipneus (drip edge) proposal above the ventilation grilles — new point J

Sven Broes asked (reply-to-all) to add the Etanco contact and any other relevant parties.

Raphaëlle replied internally (09/06, to Remco, cc Raphaël + Nicolas): available all three days (15/06 ≥10h, 18/06, 19/06), but questions why a visio is needed — in her reading the outstanding administration is already clear at the end of Equans's own email and in the prior Sites correspondence.

Nicolas's position (drafted, not yet sent): available 18/06 and 19/06; does not speak Dutch (can follow in FR or EN), needs translation of technical points if the meeting runs in Dutch. Frames the remarks as technical justifications tied to the CSC and Eurocodes, not administrative formalities.

TF-2101 (accepté avec remarques): three minor points, flexible on form — C18 ↔ S8 confirmation (one line from manufacturer suffices); Tenco compatibility attestation for Tanalith-treated wood (substitute product with covering datasheet also acceptable); application conditions to carry into UP-4044 (administrative). None justify a discussion.

TF-2100 (refusé): little negotiating room. Note fully recomputed for 70×45, dated and signed (point A, no flexibility); separate EN 1991-1-4 wind calc with per-zone, per-building pressures (Infrabel contractual imposition, point B); support-wall characteristics for all 11 buildings — prédalle type, concrete class & age, reinforcement at fixations (point F, summary table suffices); EXE plans with spacings and edge zones (contractual); ETA for FM-X3 — German agrément does not cover the concrete-side anchorage which is the failure mode (point E); demonstration that plug load stays under tested resistance (point C, follows naturally from the recomputed note, no separate document needed if covered).

Discipline holds: lead with contractual hooks (CSC / PTR CE01 / Eurocodes), burden of proof on contractor, "verify and comply" register. "Buitensporig" framing not accepted — to be reframed as compliance requirements at any meeting. See [[Projects/101581 Boitsfort/03 — Open Points]] (points I, J added).

#### 04/06/2026 — Vault reorganization & technical findings synthesis

Nicolas finalizes organization of project vault into standard structure (00–06).

Six blocking findings identified in TF-2100 v4.0 & Marcovis rekennota:
- **A.** Un-recomputed verification (section change 35×46 → 70×45)
- **B.** Wind methodology incomplete (single value vs. EN 1991-1-4 zones)
- **C.** Pull-out test non-representative (70 mm concrete vs. 80 mm AAC design)
- **D.** SLS verification missing (eccentricity, deflection, sway)
- **E.** ETA 19/0245 absent (FM-X3 plug product approval)
- **F.** Substrate grade unknown (concrete & AAC quality not documented)

Findings documented in [[Projects/101581 Boitsfort/03 — Open Points]]. Formal response to Equans in preparation (to be transmitted via Raphaëlle).

### April 2026

#### April 2026 — NC03 Non-conformité registered (works stoppage)

Site observation (Raphaël Verraghen): first buildings (NU5 pilot) show non-conformities:
- Latte-to-insulation distances exceed tolerance (> 5 mm; CSC requires ≤ 5 mm)
- Enduit (base coat) incomplete on lattage before cladding placement
- Support holes placed randomly (non-coordination)
- Screws hammered instead of properly screwed (pre-drilling requirement CSC §07.04.15)

Non-conformité sheet NC03 raised by Remco Desmet (CCM), transmitted by Raphaëlle to Equans.

Works stopped on NU5 and subsequent buildings pending resolution. NC03 covers workmanship & document management; does not address structural calculation deficiencies (separate ground for Nicolas's review).
