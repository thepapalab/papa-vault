## 2026

### June 2026

#### 04/06/2026 — Vault reorganization & technical findings synthesis

Nicolas finalizes organization of project vault into standard structure (00–06).

Six blocking findings identified in TF-2100 v4.0 & Marcovis rekennota:
- **A.** Un-recomputed verification (section change 35×46 → 70×45)
- **B.** Wind methodology incomplete (single value vs. EN 1991-1-4 zones)
- **C.** Pull-out test non-representative (70 mm concrete vs. 80 mm AAC design)
- **D.** SLS verification missing (eccentricity, deflection, sway)
- **E.** ETA 19/0245 absent (FM-X3 plug product approval)
- **F.** Substrate grade unknown (concrete & AAC quality not documented)

Findings documented in [[03 — Open Points]]. Formal response to Equans in preparation (to be transmitted via Raphaëlle).

### April 2026

#### April 2026 — NC03 Non-conformité registered (works stoppage)

Site observation (Raphaël Verraghen): first buildings (NU5 pilot) show non-conformities:
- Latte-to-insulation distances exceed tolerance (> 5 mm; CSC requires ≤ 5 mm)
- Enduit (base coat) incomplete on lattage before cladding placement
- Support holes placed randomly (non-coordination)
- Screws hammered instead of properly screwed (pre-drilling requirement CSC §07.04.15)

Non-conformité sheet NC03 raised by Remco Desmet (CCM), transmitted by Raphaëlle to Equans.

Works stopped on NU5 and subsequent buildings pending resolution. NC03 covers workmanship & document management; does not address structural calculation deficiencies (separate ground for Nicolas's review).
