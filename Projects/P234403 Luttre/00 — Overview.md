# P234403 — Luttre Sous-station de traction · Overview

> [!warning] Situation as of 04/06/2026
> **Phase as-built — final verification mode.** Four buildings (B01–B03 transformers, TFO utility) substantially complete. **15 technical blocages remain open** across radiers (A1–A6), TFO roof (B1–B6), and bardage B03 (C1–C3). As-built file cannot close until all resolved.

## Project Scope & Design

**P234403 / Poste de sectionnement de Luttre / BAT 5565 + BAT 5296**

New traction substation housing electrical equipment for lines 117 (Luttre/Braine-le-Comte), 124 (Charleroi-Sud/Bruxelles-Midi), 124A (Charleroi-Sud/Luttre). Four container-type buildings on raft foundations:
- **B01, B02, B03** — Transformer chambers: masonry walls + RC ring beams, prestressed hourdis roofs, REI 120, 30 kN/m² transformer load on TFO only
- **TFO** — Utility building: demountable roof concept disputed, 75 mm roof offset for equipment access

**Defining structural parameters:** Raft foundations cnom = 45 mm (PTR CE01 §5.6.1, EE3); masonry walls (*cellenbetonblokken*); RC ring beams; prestressed hourdis.
