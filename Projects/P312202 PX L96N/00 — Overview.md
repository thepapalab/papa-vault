# P312202 — PX L96N / Accès Atelier Forêt · Overview

> [!info] Situation as of 02/06/2026
> Prefeasibility study of a level-crossing overpass (PX) between L96N and the access track to the *Atelier de Forêt*. Final client presentation fixed 24/06/2026. Two variants under study. Cost estimation in progress (Bart De Pauw).

## Project Scope & Design

In the context of the capacity saturation declaration at Brussels-Midi station, I-CBE.35 mandated TUC RAIL to evaluate the technical conceptual feasibility of a PX between L96N and the access track to the *Atelier de Forêt*, in order to limit conflicts (*cisaillements*) between international trains and lines L28, L50A, and A-L96.

**Defining parameters:** Prefeasibility — no formal calculations. Objective: select variant and produce order-of-magnitude cost estimate. Two structural zones: Zone A (~30–60 m, concrete arch) and Zone B (~20 m, box girder or equivalent).

## Phase

*Faisabilité* — prefeasibility study.
