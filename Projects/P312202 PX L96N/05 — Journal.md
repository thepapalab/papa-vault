## 2026-06-02 — Cost estimation meeting

**Present:** Bart De Pauw (structures expert), Laurent Vercammen (Design Manager), Nicolas Papalabros.

**Subject:** First indicative presentation of cost references by Bart De Pauw.

**Key points:**
- Mono-pile bored piles = low-cost solution.
- Steel box girder: economically very sensitive. Production divided by ~10 vs. historical. Old prices not representative. Width sensitivity strong (up to +140%). Handle with caution.
- Alternative to study: excavation with shoring + micropiles — cheaper and simpler than bored piles in constrained context.
- Reference projects presented indicatively: 50A-03059, 129301 Haren, Viaduc Luctbal, 211301.
- Bart provides price references this week.
- François Lapy (TUC RAIL) completed a similar Zone B project (~20 m span). **Must be contacted.**
