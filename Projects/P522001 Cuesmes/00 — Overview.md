## Project Scope & Design

Large-scale railway workshop and infrastructure development at Cuesmes for SNCB, in coordination with Infrabel. Project number 522001. Markets: 522301 and 522302. TUC Rail structural scope — confirmed in meeting 09/06:

- **Encuvement (Laborex system)** — the Laborex system is a container housing tanks and other machinery. Structural scope: (1) supporting slab for the Laborex container, and (2) encuvement / *espace technique* — a *vide aéré* underground box through which various pipes and impétrants will pass. SNCB must provide a canalization plan (*plan de principe*) — Reginald has committed to provide this. Possible need for a *puit de terre aux abords de la dalle Laborex* — to be investigated (check if covered in SNCB template).
- **Dalle de déraillement** — derailment containment slab. Example provided by Laurent (09/06). Section with *traverse bi-bloque* identified as best option. Must be implemented in drawings; clash management with catenary portal foundations required.
- **Heurtoir** — buffer stop structure at dead-end track. Example with handwritten calculation note provided (09/06). Design approach to be reviewed and developed.
- **Fondations de portiques caténaires** — RC pad foundations for catenary portals. Steel by Jeremy Precel (T-ELE); foundation design (EC7 + EC2), AT and métré by Nicolas. Waiting for base reactions to proceed. Senior input may be required for foundation type selection.
