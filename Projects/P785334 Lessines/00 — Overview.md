## Project Scope & Design

**P785334 / TR785334 · HERMES ref.: FUWP785334-110235597**

Suppression of the level crossing at Lessines station. Construction of an underpass (*couloir sous-voies* CSV 5267) in prefabricated reinforced concrete, in two large elements assembled by a monolithic cast-in-place joint (*clavetage*). Raising of platforms 1 and 2-3. PMR ramps and prefabricated stairs (20+ elements), installed by 750T crane. Catenary foundations (MET3).

**Defining structural parameters:** CSV in two prefabricated elements; joint C35/45 cast in place with overlap reinforcement; exposure class EE4 WAI(0.45); concrete class OA minimum C35/45.
